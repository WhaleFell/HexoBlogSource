|bet|bet|bet|打赌|
|cost|cost|cost|耗费 (钱)|
|cut|cut|cut|切，割|
|hit|hit|hit|撞，击打|
|hurt|hurt|hurt|使…伤痛|
|let|let|let|让|
|put|put|put|放|

|set|set|set|安排，安置|
